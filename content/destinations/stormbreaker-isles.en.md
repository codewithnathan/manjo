itinerary: >
  **Day 1: Arrival in Tórshavn**


  Arrive in the Faroese capital, check into hotel, and evening walking tour of the old town.

  Visit Múlafossur waterfall, hike to Trælanípa cliff, and explore Gásadalur village.


  **Day 3: Northern Islands**


  Boat trip to Kalsoy island, hike to Kallur lighthouse, and visit traditional villages.


  **Day 4: Streymoy Exploration**


  Hike to Saksun village, visit Tjørnuvík beach, and evening in Tórshavn.


  **Day 5: Mykines Island**


  Day trip to Mykines for puffin watching and coastal hiking.


  **Day 6: Departure**


  Morning at leisure and departure.
